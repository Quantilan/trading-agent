# Refund Policy

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

## 1. General Policy

### 1.1 No Refunds (Standard)

Due to the digital and automated nature of our service, **all sales are final**. We do not offer refunds for:
- Subscription fees (monthly or otherwise)
- Partial subscription periods
- Unused subscription time
- Service interruptions under 24 hours
- User errors or misconfigurations
- Market losses from trading

### 1.2 Why No Refunds?

- Immediate access to software and strategies
- Digital product cannot be "returned"
- Service is consumed continuously
- Prevents abuse and exploitation

## 2. Exceptions

### 2.1 Technical Failures

If we cannot provide the service due to **our technical failure** for more than 48 consecutive hours, you may request:
- Pro-rated refund for downtime
- Service credit for next month
- Account cancellation with refund

**To qualify:**
- Issue must be on our end (not your infrastructure)
- You must have reported the issue
- We must be unable to resolve within 48 hours

### 2.2 Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice for the same subscription period due to our error, we will refund the duplicate charge within 7 business days.

### 2.3 Unauthorized Charges

If your payment was unauthorized or fraudulent, contact us immediately. We will investigate and refund if confirmed.

### 2.4 Legal Requirements

Where local law mandates refund rights (EU consumers, etc.), we comply with applicable regulations.

## 3. How to Request a Refund

### 3.1 Process

1. **Contact Support**: Message @quantilan_support
2. **Provide Details**: 
   - Your Telegram username
   - Transaction ID
   - Reason for refund request
   - Evidence (screenshots, logs)
3. **Review Period**: 5-7 business days
4. **Decision**: We will notify you of approval/denial
5. **Processing**: If approved, 7-14 business days

### 3.2 Required Information

- Telegram user ID or username
- Payment transaction hash/ID
- Date and amount of payment
- Detailed reason for refund
- Supporting documentation

## 4. Subscription Cancellation

### 4.1 Voluntary Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription anytime:
- Access continues until period end
- No refund for unused time
- No automatic renewal after cancellation
- Reactivation requires new payment

**To cancel:**
- Message @quantilan_support

### 4.2 Automatic Expiration

When subscription expires:
- Agent stops trading automatically
- You receive notification
- No automatic renewal (manual re-subscription required)
